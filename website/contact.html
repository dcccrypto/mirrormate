Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription through your account settings in the app, or through the Stripe Customer Portal. Visit the Profile tab ‚Üí Manage Subscription ‚Üí Cancel.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data. You can also email privacy@mirrormate.app to request account deletion.

What happens to my videos?

Videos are automatically deleted from our servers after 30 days. Analysis reports are stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Videos are processed securely and automatically deleted after 30 days. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI uses advanced machine learning models to analyze communication patterns. While highly accurate, AI analysis should be used as a coaching tool, not as a replacement for professional feedback.

What devices are supported?

MirrorMate is available for iPhone running iOS 15.0 or later. We're working on iPad and other platform support!

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis through Stripe's refund system. Please contact support@mirrormate.app with your request and reason.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We appreciate bug reports! Email support@mirrormate.app with details about the issue, your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce the bug.
